How To Order

How to Order

1. Select the product with the symbol PO. You can also click on the Preorder filter next to it    left on product search results.
2. Click the product you want to see the description and the order process period.
3. Click Preorder to order the product.
4. Fill in the order form, then click Order this Preorder Product.
5. To proceed to the shopping basket, click Pay.
6. Select the payment method and check your order again. Click Checkout.
7. On the next page, check the payment summary again and select Pay Now.
8. Make payments according to the method you have chosen.

Note

1. The seller will send the order / input delivery receipt a maximum of 1 day after time process preorder ends.
2. Preorder products cannot be combined in 1 Invoice if product purchases are made at the store different or have different pre order times.
